Our Philosophy

The Way of Sōkai

Three pillars drawn from the wisdom of slow living, guiding every touch, breath, and moment you spend with us.

Restore

Through touch and botanical alchemy, we coax the body back to its natural rhythm of ease and vitality.

Reconnect

In the quiet between breaths, find the thread that ties you to yourself, to nature, to the present moment.

Renew

Leave not just rested, but remade — carrying a new clarity that extends far beyond your time with us.

Practitioners

Meet the Therapists

Each therapist brings decades of practice and a deeply personal approach to their craft.

Yuki Tanaka

Shiatsu & Traditional Bodywork

Trained in Kyoto for twelve years under a third-generation shiatsu master. Yuki's touch is described as "listening hands" — she reads the body's story and works with it rather than against it.

Haruki Sato

Holistic Facial Therapy

A former dermatologist who found her true calling in holistic facial work. Haruki combines clinical knowledge with Japanese facial massage traditions for transformative results.

Mei Lin Chen

Breathwork & Meditation

Certified in both Zen and Vipassana traditions, Mei Lin has guided over two thousand people through their first meditation experience with remarkable gentleness and clarity.

Kenji Ota

Deep Tissue & Sports Recovery

Former physiotherapist for Japan's national rowing team. Kenji understands the mechanics of the body at an elite level and brings that precision to every session.
