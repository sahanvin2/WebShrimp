Our Story

Crafting timeless essentials since 2018

The Beginning

Born from a Passion for Craft

MAISON was founded in 2018 by a collective of artisans and designers who believed that luxury shouldn't be ostentatious — it should be intimate. We set out to create objects that become part of your daily rituals, pieces that age beautifully and carry meaning.

Every product begins with a sketch, evolves through dozens of prototypes, and is only released when it meets our uncompromising standards. We work with small, family-owned workshops across Italy, Portugal, and Japan — places where craftsmanship is a heritage, not just a process.

Our materials are sourced with equal care: full-grain leather from ethical tanneries, organic cotton from certified mills, and sustainably harvested wood. We believe the best products are those that respect both the people who make them and the planet we share.
