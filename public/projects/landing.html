FAQ

Common questions

How is Flowstate different from Notion or Linear?

Flowstate unifies docs, tasks, and collaboration into a single workspace — while Notion and Linear each solve only part of the puzzle. Our AI layer understands context across all your work, automating the busywork that tools in isolation simply can't do.

Can I import my existing data?

Absolutely. We have one-click importers for Notion, Linear, Asana, Jira, Trello, and GitHub Projects. Most teams are fully migrated in under 30 minutes.

Is my data secure?

Security is foundational, not an afterthought. We're SOC 2 Type II certified, use end-to-end encryption for all data in transit and at rest, and offer data residency options for Enterprise customers. We never train AI models on your data.

What happens after the 14-day trial?

You'll automatically be moved to the Starter (free) plan. No charges, no surprises. All your data is preserved — you just lose access to Pro features until you upgrade.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes, zero lock-in. Cancel from your dashboard in two clicks. You'll keep access until the end of your billing period, and we'll help you export everything if you decide to leave.

Do you offer discounts for startups or open source?

Yes! We offer 50% off for startups with less than $1M in funding, and free Pro plans for qualifying open-source maintainers. Reach out to hello@flowstate.io for details.
