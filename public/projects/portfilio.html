About Me

Design is how I
make sense of things

I'm a creative director and designer based in New York, with over a decade of experience building brands and digital products for startups, agencies, and Fortune 500 companies.

My approach is rooted in strategic thinking — every visual decision serves a purpose. I believe the best design doesn't just look good, it creates meaning, sparks emotion, and drives action.

When I'm not pushing pixels, you'll find me exploring analog photography, curating my vinyl collection, or getting lost in the architecture of a new city.
