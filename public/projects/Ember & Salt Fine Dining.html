"We do not tame the fire. We listen to it. Every log of birch and oak writes a different chapter on the plate."

At Ember & Salt, our kitchen is anchored by a three-ton hearth. No gas, no electric burners. Just the ancient dialogue between wood, salt, and time. We source exclusively from the rugged coastlines and forgotten forests within a 50-mile radius.

Led by Chef Elias Thorne, our team explores the nuances of smoke—from the aggressive sear of a direct flame to the whisper-soft perfume of cold-smoking over applewood. It is primal, yet precise.
