Frequently Asked Questions

How hard is it to migrate from existing tools?

Most of our customers fully migrate within 48 hours. We offer one-click integrations for Snowflake, BigQuery, and Postgres, and our migration utility automatically translates common dbt models.

Do you store our data?

Meridian acts as an intelligence layer. For Starter and Growth plans, we cache aggregated query results for performance. For Enterprise, we offer VPC peering where data never leaves your environment.

Can non-technical users create reports?

Absolutely. While engineers can write raw SQL, our visual builder allows marketing, sales, and product teams to drag-and-drop dimensions and metrics to build their own dashboards.
